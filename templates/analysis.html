Why Random Forest Wins

In medical diagnostics, **Recall** is often the most critical metric. A high recall means the model is very effective at identifying true positive cases (patients who actually are at high risk), thereby minimizing false negatives. Missing a diagnosis (false negative) is generally far more dangerous than preventative over-caution (false positive).
